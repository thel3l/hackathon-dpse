November 7th 2015. Be ready.

The Event

Great minds talk ideas. Greater ones still execute them.

Welcome, fellow hacker. We at DPS East are thrilled to unveil DPSE CodeFest 2015. We hope you will join us on the 7th November to create something new and innovative. The goal of CodeFest '15 is to create a discourse of ideas. We want to host a hackathon where ideas and innovations flow free.

Participants will be given a theme (will be announced a few days before the fest). Using any platform or language of their preference, they will be expected to develop something new and useful. GUI isn't a big criteria. The best ideas are simple- be creative. Think out of the box.

We recommend that fellow hackers check out and get used to version control systems like Git which we plan to use during the hackathon. However, you may also use Mercurial.
