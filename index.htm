December 19th 2015. Be ready.

The Event

Great minds talk ideas. Greater ones still execute them.

Welcome, fellow hacker. We at DPS East are thrilled to unveil DPSE CodeFest 2015. We hope you will join us on the 19th of December to create something new and innovative. The goal of CodeFest '15 is to create a discourse of ideas. It's a great opportunity for code enthusiasts to network. We want to host a hackathon where ideas and innovations flow free.

Participants will be given a theme (will be announced a few days before the fest). Using any platform or language of their preference, they will be expected to develop something new and useful. GUI isn't a big criteria. The best ideas are simple- be creative. Think out of the box.

We recommend that fellow hackers check out and get used to version control systems like Git. We plan to host a local git server for participants to collaborate on. Even after the CodeFest is over, enthusiasts can continue working and collaborate with other participants on their projects.

Agenda

Here's a brief schedule of DPSE Codefest '15. Hover over the boxes to learn more.

  • Arrival
    Until 8:00
    Participants arrive at DPS East and register.
  • Keynote
    8:00 - 8:25

  • Set up

  • Coding
    8:45 - 17:30

    Don't worry. You can have lunch whenever you want. Or not at all. Our food sponsors will begin serving hackers at 12:45.

  • Presentation
    17:30 - 19:00
    Hackers present their creations before the judges.
  • Closure
    19:00 - 19:30
    The best of the hacks get awarded.
