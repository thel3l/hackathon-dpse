The Event

Welcome, fellow hacker. We at DPS East are thrilled to unveil DPSE CodeFest 2015. We implore you to join us on 30th October to create something new and innovative.
Great minds talk ideas. Greater ones still execute them. The goal of CodeFest '15 is to create a discourse of ideas. We want to host a hackathon where ideas and innovations flow free.
We don't judge based on platform or language. The best of ideas are simple. A beautiful GUI is not going to change an unoriginal concept.

Rules

You can have a maximum of 5 members in a team.

To ensure a level field for all contestants, all code, design, art, music and assets MUST be created during the duration of the Hackathon. We want to ensure that all participants start off on the same footing and we also want to preserve the true nature of a hackathon. The only exception to this rule would include material that is freely available to the public like Creative Commons Images and third party apis.

Winning submissions may be subject to code review. Be kind to others. Do not insult or put down other attendees. Behave professionally. Remember that harassment and racist, sexist, or exclusionary jokes are not appropriate for this event. Hack, meet new people, get creative, and have a good time. Getting something meaningful done within such a short amount of time can be stressful, but we'd like to encourage everyone to take a break every now and then to relax and enjoy the event.

What you'll need

Don't forget to bring the following items for the hackathon:

  • Laptops
  • Smartphones
  • Chargers
  • Internet Dongles
  • Ideas
  • A backpack
  • Extention board
  • Notebook and pen
  • Flash drives / Hard drives
